Balancing player type:

1. Killer - killer element is represented by cleaning the nuclear residual ( killing the nuclear blobs)

2. Achiever - this element is represented by getting the highest score

3. Socializer - players talking about how to beat the higher difficulty levels

4. Explorer - the learning experience is the most representative for this player type
